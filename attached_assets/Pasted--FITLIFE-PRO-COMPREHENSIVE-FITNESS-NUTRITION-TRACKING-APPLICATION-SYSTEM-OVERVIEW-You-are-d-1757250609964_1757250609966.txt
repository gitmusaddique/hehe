# FITLIFE PRO - COMPREHENSIVE FITNESS & NUTRITION TRACKING APPLICATION

## SYSTEM OVERVIEW
You are designing FitLife Pro, a billion-dollar fitness tracking application that combines exercise and nutrition management. The app must be production-ready, user-friendly for non-technical users, and utilize SQLite3 for local data storage with lightweight ML capabilities for predictions.

## MARKET ANALYSIS DIRECTIVE
Based on competitive research: MyFitnessPal leads in nutrition tracking with barcode scanning capabilities, Nike Training Club excels in guided workouts, and Strava dominates GPS-based fitness tracking with social features. Your application must integrate and surpass all these functionalities in a unified platform.
